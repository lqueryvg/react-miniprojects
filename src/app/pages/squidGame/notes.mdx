## Exercise

Write a simple game as follows:

- display a box randomly toggles between green and red
- the goal is to click 15 times on the green box within 15 seconds
- the player loses if they click red or they run out of time
- each colour is displayed for somewhere between 1 and 2 seconds (randomly
  within these limits)
